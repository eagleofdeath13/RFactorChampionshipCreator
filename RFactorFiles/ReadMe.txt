About the Game:
===============

rFactor (tm) is a racing game featuring the latest graphics technology, excellent multiplayer, fine vehicle dynamics, and support for a wide variety of racing formats.  rFactor ships with two race series - the SR Grand Prix which includes a range of road cars and the Open Wheel Challenge which features dedicated racing vehicles from 4 to 10 cylinders.  Both series include a mix of oval, road, and street courses.

See readme.chm for the latest rFactor news and updates.
